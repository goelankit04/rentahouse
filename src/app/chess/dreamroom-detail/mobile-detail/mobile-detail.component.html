Topics to be covered include

Opening Principles

How to prepare an opening by oneself

How to form an opening repertoire over a period of time

How to handle dynamic and static positions

The art of calculation

Prophylaxis

Role of exchanges

Exploiting weakness

Handling of pieces

Playing on colors

Endgame technique

suitable for
1. Beginners:

5 hours of quality content aimed towards people who want to learn chess from scratch

2. Intermediate:

(1100 - 2000 FIDE Rating): 20 hours of content

3. Advanced:

(2000+ FIDE Rating): 15 hours of content

Special attention will be given to psychological issues that affect a players growth(e.g. time pressure, low self-confidence, fear of losing a game, fear of rating fluctuations etc.)
